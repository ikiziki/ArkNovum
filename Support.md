# Support ArkNovum

## Help Preserve The Things We Create

ArkNovum is built around a simple idea:

> The things we create, collect, and dedicate our time to deserve to be preserved.

Building and maintaining an open, self-hosted platform takes time, infrastructure, development effort, documentation, and community support.

If ArkNovum helps you organize your collections, document your projects, or preserve your hobby journey, consider supporting the project.

Every contribution helps keep ArkNovum growing.

# Pro Plugins

ArkNovum will remain free to use as a self-hosted hobby archive platform.

Future Pro plugins may be offered to provide advanced features, specialized workflows, and additional integrations.

Examples of potential Pro features:

- Advanced analytics
- AI-assisted organization
- Premium integrations
- Expanded automation
- Specialized hobby tools
- Enhanced collection management

Revenue from Pro plugins helps support:

- Continued development
- Infrastructure costs
- Security improvements
- Documentation
- Community resources

# Transparency

Support helps ArkNovum continue development, but the project will always remain focused on its original purpose:

Preserving the stories behind the things we build.

The goal is to create a sustainable project while keeping hobby archiving accessible to everyone.
